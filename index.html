about.

VorteK created the server in 27/6/16, and started meeting people, making friends and making a community to what it is today.

This community was founded with the sole purpose of providing a haven for like minded gamers. Come forward in time when VorteK started social media dating as back as 2014 till present along with repurposing the discord with a better vision and making it safer from all kinds of bad elements. The name "academy" came as an alternative for the community name from the usual "army" which many content creators use. With that came the idea of making a school themed discord community.
